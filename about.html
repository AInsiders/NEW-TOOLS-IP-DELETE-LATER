About A.Insiders

A.Insiders is a cutting-edge technology company that evolved from CellCurity, a mobile phone solution company formed in 2016 when Blake Zimmerman and Babu Narayana first met. Both founders began their technology journeys at a young age, developing their expertise independently before joining forces. With each founder bringing over 20+ years of individual experience to the partnership, our founders have transformed the company into A.Insiders in 2023, expanding our focus to include AI, cybersecurity, and comprehensive technology solutions. We specialize in developing Android applications, news platforms, and security solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital landscape.

Our mission is to create technology solutions that not only meet today's challenges but anticipate tomorrow's opportunities. From our flagship news application to our upcoming security tools, every product we develop is designed with the user's privacy, security, and experience at the forefront.
